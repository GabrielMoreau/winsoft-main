# DigiCamControl - Control your camera

DigiCamControl is free, open-source software that lets you control your
camera's settings remotely from your Windows PC via USB. You can take
photos using the camera's shutter button or remotely from your computer.
By holding the camera in your hand, you can take photos and then view
the resulting images on your computer screen.

* Website : https://digicamcontrol.com/

* Download : https://digicamcontrol.com/download,
  https://sourceforge.net/projects/digicamcontrol/files/
